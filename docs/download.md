## Download

* [Blender Extensions](https://extensions.blender.org/approval-queue/btt/)
* [GitHub Release](https://github.com/moixllik/btt/releases/tag/latest)

![install disk](./images/install-disk.png)

* Download `btt-*.zip`
* Open Blender 4.2+
* `Edit / Preferences / Get Extensions`
* `Install from Disk`
